Creamy Tuscan Sausage and Kale Soup

Return to Home Page

Description

A cozy, comforting soup made with savory Italian sausage, saut√©ed garlic and onion, creamy broth, tender potatoes, and hearty kale. Rich, velvety, and full of Tuscan-inspired flavor.

Ingredients List

  • 1 15oz can of white kidney beans
  • 48oz of chicken broth
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 4oz heavy whipping cream
  • 1 bunch of kale
  • 4 pork sausages
  • 8-10 small red potatoes
  • 1 medium white onion
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • Itlian seasoning
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Slice the length of the sausages. Peel off the casing and discard casing.
  3. Add oil to heated pot. Add pork and break apart. Cook until brown and crumbled for about 6-8 minutes.
  4. Peel and mince garlic. Peel and dice onion.
  5. Add garlic and onion to pot, stirring frequently for about 4-5 minutes.
  6. Drain and rinse beans.
  7. Add beans, broth, and spices to the pot
  8. Wash potatoes. Cut into about 1/8in pieces. Add to the pot.
  9. Wash and dry kale.
  10. Chop or tear leaves into about bite sized pieces. Add to the pot in handfuls.
  11. Let soup simmer, covered, until potatoes are fork tender. About 10-15 minutes. Add cream and stir to combine.
  12. Serve and enjoy!
