Easy Turkey Chili

Return to Home Page

Description

This cozy turkey chili is a super simple, big-batch classic perfect for the colder months. Mildly seasoned and not too spicy, it leans on everyday pantry ingredients and straightforward steps. Nothing fancy. It may not win any chili cook-offs, but it reliably delivers hearty, crowd-pleasing comfort in every bowl. Great for meal prep, leftovers, or feeding a hungry group with minimal effort.

Ingredients List

  • 3lbs of 85% lean turkey
  • 2 15oz cans of red kidney beans
  • 2 cans of rotel
  • 4-8oz of spicy chicken broth (as needed for consistency)
  • 1 large diced white onion
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • Chili Powder
  • Cumin
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Two jars of your choice of salsa
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Olive Oil

Steps

Brown and Season Meat

Combine into the Crock Pot
